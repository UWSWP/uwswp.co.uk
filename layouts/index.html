MEET JAMES LE GRICE

James Le Grice, a dedicated and accomplished swimmer, began his competitive journey at the age of 8. After a brief hiatus, he rediscovered his passion for swimming upon joining Warwick.

Currently pursuing a BSc in Mathematics, James aspires to forge a career in the finance industry. His analytical mindset, honed through academic pursuits, complements his swimming endeavors, driving him to constantly refine his skills and strive for excellence both in and out of the pool.

Noteworthy accomplishments:
  • 8th British Summer Championships 2022 (50m Butterfly)
  • 22nd British Swimming Commonwealth Trials 2022 (50m Butterfly)
  • 5th BUCS Long Course (50m Backstroke)

ATHLETE HIGHLIGHT

MEET ISOBEL JAMES

Isobel James, a skilled water polo player, represented the University of Warwick's women's first team while pursuing her BSc in Economics. With a background in swimming, Isobel seamlessly transitioned into water polo, finding the sport to be a natural fit. Under the guidance of supportive coaches and experienced teammates, she developed her skills and thrived in the training sessions.

Competing both in friendly matches against local clubs and in the BUCS Midlands League 1, Isobel proudly represented Team Warwick. The camaraderie within the team was exceptional, and the encouragement from the university community during matches and Varsity events was motivating. Water polo provided Isobel with not only intense training and competitive matches but also valuable opportunities to form lasting friendships and socialise with diverse individuals from across the university.

Water Polo

Water polo at Warwick offers not just high-level university competition and training, but also a fantastic social team atmosphere for players of all abilities. Our team consists of a diverse group, ranging from players competing in the British National League and European semi-professionals with youth training from top clubs worldwide, to complete novices who have never held a water polo ball before joining. Despite our varying backgrounds, we are united by a strong bond to our sport and team.
